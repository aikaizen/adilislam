Daily AI Research Briefing — March 8, 2026

OpenRouter's Hunter and Healer. Purpose-built models for agent workflows.

🎯 Hunter

Optimized for information retrieval, search, and synthesis. Long context navigation, multi-hop reasoning, source verification. Ideal for research agents.

🩹 Healer

Optimized for debugging, error correction, and code repair. Stack trace analysis, root cause identification, minimal diffs. Ideal for maintenance agents.

📊 Architecture Implication

Specialized models for specialized tasks. The future is not one model doing everything, but orchestrators routing to purpose-built subagents.
